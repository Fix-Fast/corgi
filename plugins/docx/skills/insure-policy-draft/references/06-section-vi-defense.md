SECTION VI: DEFENSE AND SETTLEMENT

1) We have the right and duty to defend any Demand covered by the Policy and such obligation is limited to the payment of amounts constituting Defense Costs. We will appoint an attorney to defend any covered Demand. We have the right, but not the duty or obligation, to investigate any Occurrence and the costs of any such investigation will be deemed Defense Costs, even if incurred prior to the existence of a Demand.

2) The Insureds will not incur any Defense Costs, retain defense counsel, settle, or offer to settle any Demand, assume any contractual obligation, admit liability, voluntarily make any payment or confess or otherwise consent to any damages or judgments with respect to any Demand without our prior written consent. We will not be liable for any Defense Costs, settlement, assumed obligation, admitted liability, voluntary payment, confessed damages, or judgments to which we have not consented in writing.

3) Our obligations under the Policy, including but not limited to our duty to defend any Demand, will end once the applicable Limit of Liability is exhausted by the payment of covered Loss, Defense Costs, and Medical Payments. Our duty and obligation to defend any Demand also will end when and if, solely at our option, we tender to an Insured, to any plaintiff or plaintiffs, or into any court of competent jurisdiction, the applicable Limit of Liability under the Policy. Such tender will not only end our duty to defend, but will constitute full satisfaction of all of our duties and obligations under the Policy with respect to that Demand. If our duty to defend ends with respect to any Demand, we will notify the Named Insured so that the Named Insured can arrange to take control of the defense of the Insureds. We will take whatever reasonable steps are necessary to avoid a default judgment during a transfer of control of the defense of any such Demand. If we do so, the Named Insured agrees to repay the reasonable fees, costs, and expenses incurred by us in excess of the applicable Limit of Liability during the transfer and further agrees that, in undertaking the steps necessary to avoid a default judgment during the transfer, we have not waived any rights under this Policy.

4) The Insureds will provide full cooperation and all information and particulars that we may request to conduct an investigation, defend a Demand, or reach a settlement of the Demand. The Insureds agree that, in the event of an Occurrence or a Demand, they will do nothing that may increase our liability, compromise any defenses, or prejudice our position or rights of recovery.

5) We may settle any Demand for any monetary amount within the available Limit of Liability that we consider reasonable, without the consent of an Insured.
