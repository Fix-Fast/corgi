# 5 — Section V: Exclusions  *(the research-heavy section)*

**Kind**: the canonical list below is boilerplate. *Per-insured customizations — additions, carve-outs, deletions — are expected here and should be driven by the user.*

**Structure of this section**: three exclusion groups
- *Exclusions Applicable to All Coverages (A, B, and C)* — 23 entries (Prior Notice → Recording/Distribution in Violation of Law)
- *Exclusions Applicable to Coverage A and Coverage C* — 9 entries (Expected/Intended → Personal & Advertising Injury)
- *Exclusions Applicable to Coverage B* — 8 entries (Material Published w/ Knowledge of Falsity → Unauthorized Use of Another's Name)

## Canonical text (insert verbatim)

SECTION V: EXCLUSIONS

We shall not be liable to pay any Loss, or to defend or continue to defend any Demand, in connection with any matter described below, regardless of whether any other cause or event contributes concurrently or in any sequence to such matter.

1) Exclusions Applicable to All Coverages (Coverage A, B, and C):

We shall not be liable to pay any Loss, Defense Costs, or Medical Payments on account of any Occurrence:

    a) Prior Notice: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, an Occurrence that any Insured, or any employee authorized to give or receive notice for the Named Insured, knew had occurred, in whole or in part, before the Policy Period. An Occurrence will be treated as known when:

        i) the Insured gives notice to us or any insurer regarding the injury or damage,

        ii) the Insured receives a demand or Demand because of it, or

        iii) the Insured otherwise becomes aware that injury or damage has taken place.

    b) Prior Conduct: Based upon, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any conduct, act, error or omission by any Insured to the extent the conduct, act, error or omission occurred:before the earlier of:

        i) when such Insured became an Insured, or

        ii) the Retroactive Date applicable to such Insured; or

        iii) after such Insured ceased to be an Insured.

2) Prior or Pending Proceedings: Based upon, arising out of, or in any way involving any demand, suit, or other proceeding pending against any Insured on or prior to the applicable Retroactive Date stated in the Declarations, or the same or substantially the same fact, circumstance or situation underlying or alleged in such prior demand, suit, or other proceeding.

3) Electronic Data: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, loss of, loss of use of, damage to, corruption of, inability to access, or inability to manipulate electronic data.

4) Asbestos and Lead: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, actual or alleged inhalation of, ingestion of, contact with, exposure to, existence of, or presence of asbestos or lead in any form, or any obligation of the Insured to indemnify any party because of such claims, or to share damages with or repay someone else who must pay damages related to such matters.

5) Workers’ Compensation: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any obligation of the Insured under a workers’ compensation, disability benefits, unemployment compensation, or similar law.

6) Employer’s Liability: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Bodily Injury or Property Damage to:

    a) an Employee of the Insured arising out of and in the course of employment by the Insured, or performing duties related to the conduct of the Insured’s business; or

    b) the spouse, child, parent, brother or sister of that Employee as a consequence of the injury in (a) above.

This exclusion applies whether the Insured may be liable as an employer or in any other capacity, and whether the liability is direct or vicarious.

7) Professional Services: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the rendering of or failure to render professional services by the Insured, including but not limited to any health care, legal, accounting, architectural, engineering, consulting, or insurance services, or any advice, instruction, design, or plan provided as part of such services.

8) Contractual Liability: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, liability for which the Insured is obligated to pay by reason of the assumption of liability in a written contract or agreement. This exclusion does not apply to liability for Loss that the Insured would have in the absence of the contract or agreement.

9) Employment-Related Practices: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part:

    a) any employment-related practice, policy, act or omission (such as coercion, demotion, evaluation, reassignment, discipline, defamation, harassment, humiliation, discrimination, termination, or malicious prosecution, or inappropriate employment hiring or promotion decisions); or

    b) the spouse, child, parent, brother, sister or other relative of that person as a consequence of injury to the person at whom any of the employment-related practices described above is directed. This exclusion applies whether the Insured may be held liable as an employer or in any other capacity and to any obligation to share damages with or repay someone else who must pay damages because of the injury.

10) Fraud and Intentional Acts: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any fraudulent, dishonest, criminal or malicious act, error or omission, or willful violation of law by an Insured. However, this exclusion shall only apply following a final, non-appealable adjudication in a proceeding separate from any coverage proceeding establishing that such Insured committed, participated in, or had knowledge of such conduct. In the event this exclusion is determined to apply, the Insured shall reimburse us for any Defense Costs and Loss previously advanced or paid by us in connection with the excluded Demand.

11) Medicare/Medicaid: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any actual or alleged fraud, abuse, or non-compliance with Medicare/Medicaid regulations.

12) Illegal Profit: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any Insured gaining profit, remuneration or financial advantage to which such party was not legally entitled.

13) Business Dispute: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any business dispute, including but not limited to any: interference with business relations; interference with contract; breach of contract; interference with prospective business advantage; unfair competition; unlawful, unfair, or fraudulent business practices; unfair, deceptive, untrue, or misleading advertising; misappropriation of trade secrets; or other unfair trade practice or business practice.

14) Cyber Liability: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any cyber liability, including but not limited to any: network security breach, hacker attack, computer virus, theft of electronic data, denial of service attack, theft or loss of personally identifiable non-public data, failure/malfunction/inadequacy of any electronic data processing equipment or media, or data extortion costs.

15) Record Amendments: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any records that have been amended, appended, deleted, corrected, falsified, edited, altered, or modified not in accordance with generally accepted standards by any Insured, at any Insured’s direction, or by any person for whose acts or omissions any Insured is legally responsible.

16) Discrimination: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any actual or alleged discrimination of any kind, including but not limited to allegations of harassment, civil rights violations or any other discrimination based on race, color, creed, age, gender, sexual preference or orientation, national origin, physical handicap, HIV and AIDS status, disability, or marital status.

17) Weapons: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the ownership, manufacture, import, export, sale, distribution, transfer, gift, loan, licensing, entrustment, maintenance, repair, assembly, testing, display, storage, transportation, or disposal of any firearm, ammunition, explosive, or weapon, including any parts, components, attachments, or accessories.

18) Nuclear Liability: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any loss, injury, damage or legal obligation arising out of the hazardous properties of nuclear material, including but not limited to nuclear reaction, radiation, or radioactive contamination, at any time.

19) Liquor Liability: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, an Insured’s liability by reason of:

    a) causing or contributing to the intoxication of any person;

    b) furnishing alcoholic beverages to a person under the legal drinking age or under the influence of alcohol; or

    c) any statute, ordinance or regulation relating to the sale, gift, distribution or use of alcoholic beverages.

This exclusion applies even if the Demand alleges negligence or other wrongdoing in the supervision, hiring, employment, training or monitoring of others by an Insured, or providing or failing to provide transportation to any person under the influence of alcohol. However, this exclusion applies only if the Insured is in the business of manufacturing, distributing, selling, serving, or furnishing alcoholic beverages.

20) War: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, war, civil war, revolution, insurrection, rebellion, or any warlike action by a military force or other agent (including action in hindering or defending against any of these).

21) Pollution: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the actual, alleged, or threatened discharge, dispersal, seepage, migration, release, or escape of Pollutants, regardless of whether such was accidental or intentional.

22) Recording and Distribution of Material or Information in Violation of Law: Bodily Injury or Property Damage based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any action or omission that violates or is alleged to violate:

    a) the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), including any amendment or addition to such law;

    b) the CAN-SPAM Act of 2003 (Controlling the Assault of Non-Solicited Pornography and Marketing), including any amendment or addition to such law;

    c) the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) and any Fair and Accurate Credit Transactions Act (FACTA) amendment, including any amendment or addition to such law; or

    d) any federal, state or local statute, ordinance or regulation (other than the U.S. antitrust laws and federal securities laws, which are addressed elsewhere if applicable) that addresses, prohibits or limits the printing, dissemination, disposal, collecting, recording, sending, transmitting, communicating or distribution of material or information.

23) Exclusions Applicable to Coverage A and Coverage C:

We shall not be liable to pay any Loss, Defense Costs, or Medical Payments on account of any Occurrence:

    a) Expected or Intended Injury: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Bodily Injury or Property Damage expected or intended from the standpoint of any Insured. However, this exclusion does not apply to Bodily Injury resulting from the use of reasonable force to protect persons or property.

    b) Aircraft, Auto or Watercraft: Bodily Injury or Property Damage based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the ownership, maintenance, operation, use, loading or unloading or entrustment to others of any aircraft, Auto or watercraft by any Insured or on any Insured’s behalf. This exclusion applies even if the Demands against any Insured allege negligence or other wrongdoing in the supervision, hiring, employment, training, or monitoring of others by that Insured.

This exclusion does not apply to: see

        i) a watercraft while ashore on premises you own or rent;

        ii) a watercraft you do not own that is less than 26 feet long and not being used to carry persons or property for a charge;

            (1) parking of an Auto on or next to premises you own or rent, provided the Auto is not owned by or rented or loaned to you or the driver (this exception applies only to liability caused by the parking of the Auto, and not to physical damage to the Auto itself); or

            (2) Bodily Injury or Property Damage arising out of the operation of machinery or equipment that is attached to, or part of, a land vehicle that would qualify under the definition of Worksite Equipment if it were not subject to compulsory motor vehicle insurance laws. For the purposes of this exclusion, Aircraft includes any Unmanned Aircraft, drone, remotely piloted aircraft system, or similar device, including any equipment, payload, or component attached thereto.

    c) Worksite Equipment: Bodily Injury or Property Damage based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part,the transportation of Worksite Equipment by an Auto owned or operated by or rented or loaned to any Insured; or the use of Worksite Equipment in, or while in practice or preparation for, any prearranged racing, speed, demolition, or stunting activity.

        i) Damage to Property: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Property Damage to:

            (1) property you own, rent, or occupy;

            (2) premises you sell, give away or abandon, if the Property Damage arises out of any part of those premises;

            (3) property loaned to you;

            (4) personal property in the care, custody or control of any Insured;

            (5) that particular part of real property on which you or any contractors or subcontractors working on your behalf are performing operations, if the Property Damage arises out of those operations; or

                (a) that particular part of any property that must be restored, repaired or replaced because Your Work was incorrectly performed on it. However, this exclusion does not apply to: (i) Property Damage (other than by fire) to premises, including he contents of such premises, rented to you for a period of seven (7) or fewer consecutive days or (ii) Property Damage to premises you have sold, given away or abandoned, if the premises are Your Work and were never occupied, rented or held for rental by you.

    d) Damage to Your Product: Property Damage to Your Product based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Your Product itself, or any part of it.

    e) Damage to Your Work: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Property Damage to Your Work. This exclusion does not apply if the damaged work, or the work out of which the damage arises, was performed on your behalf by a subcontractor.

        i) Damage to Impaired Property or Property Not Physically Injured: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Property Damage to Impaired Property or property that has not been physically injured, arising out of:

            (1) a defect, deficiency, inadequacy or dangerous condition in Your Product or Your Work; or

            (2) a delay or failure by you or anyone acting on your behalf to perform a contract or agreement in accordance with its terms.

    f) Recall of Products, Work or Impaired Property: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the withdrawal, recall, inspection, repair, replacement, adjustment, removal or disposal of:

        i) Your Product;

        ii) Your Work; or

        iii) Impaired Property; if such product, work or property is withdrawn or recalled from the market or from use by any person or organization because of a known or suspected defect, deficiency, inadequacy or dangerous condition in it.

    g) Personal and Advertising Injury: Bodily Injury or Property Damage based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Personal and Advertising Injury.

24) Exclusions Applicable to Coverage B — Personal and Advertising Injury Liability:

We shall not be liable to pay any Loss or Defense Costs on account of any Demand:

    a) Material Published With Knowledge of Falsity: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, an oral or written publication, in any manner, of material by or at the direction of the Insured, if done with knowledge of its falsity.

    b) Material Published Prior to Policy Period: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, an oral or written publication, in any manner, of material whose first publication took place before the beginning of the Policy Period.

    c) Quality or Performance of Goods — Failure to Conform to Statements: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the failure of goods, products or services to conform with any statement of quality or performance made in your Advertisement.

    d) Wrong Description of Prices: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the wrong description of the price of goods, products or services stated in your Advertisement.

    e) Infringement of Intellectual Property: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, any actual or alleged infringement or violation of copyright, patent, trademark, trade secret, trade dress, service mark, or other intellectual property rights. However, this exclusion does not apply to infringement of copyright, trade dress or slogan in your Advertisement, to the extent that such act otherwise falls within the Personal and Advertising Injury offenses covered by this Policy.

    f) Insureds in Media and Internet Type Businesses: Based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, Personal and Advertising Injury committed by an Insured whose business is advertising, broadcasting, publishing or telecasting, or whose business involves designing or determining content of websites for others, or providing Internet search, access, content or service. This exclusion applies to Personal and

Advertising Injury arising out of the Insured’s rendering of or failure to render specialized media and internet services in the course of that

business.

    g) Electronic Chatrooms or Bulletin Boards: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, an electronic chatroom or bulletin board that the Insured hosts, owns, or exercises control over.

    h) Unauthorized Use of Another’s Name or Product: Personal and Advertising Injury based on, arising out of, or in any way involving, in whole or in part, the unauthorized use of another’s name or product in an e-mail address, domain name, metatag, keyword or other similar tactics aimed at misleading another’s potential customers.

25) Exclusions Applicable to Coverage C — Medical Expenses Liability:

We shall not be liable to pay any Medical Payment on account of:

    a) Employee Injury: We are not obligated to pay Medical Payments to any Insured, to any person hired by any Insured, by any Insured’s tenant, or to a person injured on that part of premises the person normally occupies.
