SECTION II: INSURING AGREEMENTS

Coverage A — Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability

We agree to pay Loss and Defense Costs an Insured must legally pay as a result of a Demand for Bodily Injury or Property Damage, provided that the Bodily Injury or Property Damage is caused by an Occurrence during the Policy Period and within the Coverage Territory.

Coverage B — Personal and Advertising Injury Liability

We agree to pay Loss and Defense Costs an Insured must legally pay as a result of a Demand for Personal and Advertising Injury, provided that the Personal and Advertising Injury is caused by an Occurrence during the Policy Period and within the Coverage Territory.

Coverage C — Medical Expenses

We agree to pay Medical Expenses because of Bodily Injury caused by an Occurrence on premises you own or rent or arising from your operations, provided the Occurrence is within the Coverage Territory during the Policy Period and Medical Expenses are incurred and reported within one year. The injured person must submit to examinations we reasonably request, at our expense.
