SECTION IV: LIMITATIONS OF LIABILITY AND RETENTIONS

1) Limits of Liability

    a) Policy Aggregate Limit of Liability: The Policy Aggregate stated in the Declarations is the maximum amount we will pay under this Policy for all Loss, Defense Costs, and Medical Payments. In no event will we pay more than the Policy Aggregate. Interrelated Occurrences shall be deemed to constitute one single Occurrence for the purposes of determining the applicable limits of liability of this Policy and shall be deemed to have first occurred at the earliest point any such portion of an Interrelated Occurrence has occurred.

    b) If the limits of liability for any provision of this CGL Policy are exhausted by payment of Loss, judgments, settlements, Defense Costs or Medical Payments, our obligations under this Policy shall be deemed completely fulfilled and extinguished.

    c) The amount listed as the Per Occurrence Limit of Liability for Insuring Agreement A, Insuring Agreement B, and Insuring Agreement C of the Declarations is the maximum aggregate amount of Loss, Defense Costs, and Medical Payments we will pay under the Policy for each Occurrence under Insuring Agreement A, Insuring Agreement B, or Insuring Agreement C, and is further reduced by the amount of any applicable Retention.

    d) Our liability under this CGL Policy with respect to all Defense Costs, Loss, and Medical Payments arising from any single Occurrence shall apply only in excess of the Retention set forth in the Declarations. The Retention amount shall be your uninsured responsibility.

    e) Products-Completed Operations Aggregate Limit: The Products-Completed Operations Aggregate Limit is the most we will pay under Coverage A for Loss and Defense Costs because of Bodily Injury or Property Damage included within the Products-Completed Operations Hazard.

    f) Damage to Premises Rented To You Limit: Subject to 2. above, the Damage To Premises Rented To You Limit is the most we will pay under Coverage A for Loss because of Property Damage to any one premises while rented to you, or (in the case of damage by fire) while rented to you or temporarily occupied by you with the owner’s permission.

    g) Defense Costs Erosion: Defense Costs are part of, and operate to reduce or erode, the applicable Limit of Liability, unless otherwise noted by Endorsement to this Policy.

    h) The Limit of Liability of this Policy applies separately to each consecutive annual period and to any remaining period of less than 12 months in the Policy Period, unless the Policy Period is extended after issuance for an additional period less than 12 months. In that case, the additional period will be deemed part of the last preceding period for purposes of applying the Limit of Liability.

2) Retention

The Retention is the amount shown in the Declarations and is the amount that shall apply to each and every Occurrence during the Policy Period. Our liability to pay any Loss and Defense Costs under this Policy applies only to that part of Loss and Defense Costs which is in excess of the Retention amount stated in the Declarations. The Retention shall be borne by you and shall remain uninsured. You are responsible for payment of the Retention for each Demand. If a single Occurrence or offense results in Demands that trigger more than one coverage (Coverage A, B, or C) under this Policy, only one Retention (the highest applicable Retention) shall apply to all Loss arising from that Occurrence or offense. In no event will we be obligated to pay any amounts (including Defense Costs or Loss) until the Retention has been satisfied by payments made by you. Payment of the Retention does not reduce the Limits of Liability available under this Policy. If any covered Loss within the Retention is paid on behalf of an Insured by a source other than you, such payment will reduce the Retention to the extent of that payment. However, payments made by us or on our behalf do not satisfy or reduce the Retention. If we advance any amounts that are within the Retention (with no obligation to do so), you agree to reimburse us for such amounts as soon as practicable. You shall not settle, offer to settle, or otherwise resolve any Demand, including any settlement within the applicable Retention, without our prior written consent. Any settlement entered into without such consent shall not erode the Retention, shall not be covered under this Policy, and may, at our option, void coverage for the related Demand.
